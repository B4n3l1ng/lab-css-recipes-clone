French Onion Soup

We have been trying French onion soup in restaurants for years and my family and friends agree none can compare to my recipe for taste and simplicity of preparation.

Ingredients


  • 1/cup unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cups of slice onions
  • 4 (10.5 ounces) cans beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons of dry sherry (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 slices French bread
  • 4 slices of provolone cheese
  • 2 slices Swiss cheese, diced
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Melt butter with olive oil in an 8 quart sotck pot on medium heat. Add onions and continually stil until tender and translucent. Do not brown the onions.
  2. Add beef broth, sherry and thyme. Season with salt and pepper, and simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat the over broiler.
  4. Ladle soup into oven safe serving bowls and place one slice of bread on top of each (bread may be broken into pieces if you prefer.) Layer each slice of bread with a slice of provolone, 1/2 slice diced Swiss and 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese. Place bowls on cookie sheet and broil in the preheated over until cheese bubbles and browns slightly.
